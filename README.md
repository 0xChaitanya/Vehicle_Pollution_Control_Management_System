Due to high air pollution in Delhi, the Government of Delhi imposed a strict rule requiring vehicles without a valid PUCC (Pollution Under Control Certificate) to be denied refuelling. The Government of Delhi plans to build a centralised **DATABASE** to manage information on PUCC distribution, PUCC vendors, fuel stations, and vehicles operating in Delhi. This system involves the Government of Delhi transport department, private PUCC vendors, fuel stations and vehicles and their owners. Vehicle owners apply for new or renewed PUCCs; private vendors or the transport department conduct pollution testing and issue certificates; and fuel stations check PUCCs and submit the number of PUCC-equipped vehicles to the government. The government monitors vendors, owners and fuel stations.
